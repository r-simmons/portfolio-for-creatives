Healthcare Plus

There are currently significant barriers faced by transgender persons when it comes to healthcare in Canada. There is a disproportionate amount of violence, stigma, and misinformation that has a direct negative impact on the lives of trans individuals.

Healthcare Plus is a 2-day conference taking place on August 11th and 12th with the main message being “Caring for the minority to help the majority.”

Current and prospective medical students need to set a new standard and no longer settle for mediocre healthcare. Thus, this campaign will seek to engage aspiring medical practitioners so that they can change this industry by adopting gender-affirming and non-discriminatory practices.
