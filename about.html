Riley Simmons is an Indigenous graphic designer working in Vancouver, BC.

A recent graduate of the IDEA School of Design at Capilano University, Riley brings diverse educational and work experience to her craft. Having worked in fashion production, marketing and Indigenous event organization, Riley is passionate about fostering community and connection. Her approach to design reflects the deep satisfaction in exploring the complex, often contradictory, nature of the human experience.

Riley is now open to working with new clients.
