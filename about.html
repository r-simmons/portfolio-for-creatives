Riley is a recent Capilano University graduate and is working as a graphic designer in Vancouver BC. With a diverse educational background in digital design, fashion production, and marketing from institutions like Capilano University, Robert Gordon University and Vancouver Community College, combined with impactful work experiences, Riley is passionate about connections and fostering community. From leading marketing initiatives at TD Bank to organizing Indigenous events and mentoring peers, Riley has developed strong leadership and collaboration skills.


Riley deeply understands community and advocacy, which further influences her creative direction. From a philosophical perspective, Riley strives to create tension by juxtaposing contrasting imagery and topics, crafting innovative and thought-provoking outcomes that highlight the complex, often contradictory, nature of the human experience. always driven by a desire to uplift and inspire others through creativity, advocacy, and community engagement.
