Once upon a time, in the rainy city of Vancouver, a young adventurer named Riley began her creative journey. With heritage rooted in the tranquil landscapes of Hagwilget Village in Northern BC—"the place of quiet people"—Riley embraced her rich Indigenous identity, even if quiet was never her strong suit.

Her quest took her to the IDEA School of Design at Capilano University and the fashion workshops of Vancouver Community College. But her greatest lessons came from the world itself—wandering distant lands, immersing herself in diverse cultures, and discovering inspiration in every encounter. For Riley, the act of creating is as vibrant and transformative as the adventures that shape her.

Guided by a fascination with opposites. Riley creates designs that bridge worlds and celebrate the dichotomy of being human. Her work is a testament to the beauty found in contrasts.

When the battle of design subsides, Riley can be found on another kind of quest: to slay the mythical beast of her yearly reading goal. Each book conquered is another chapter in her ever-growing legend.
