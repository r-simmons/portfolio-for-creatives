Playing

Playing is a four-look collection created at Vancouver Community College in the Fashion Design and Production Program. Conceptualized, hand-drafted and sewn by Riley, this collection, inspired by children’s sensory toys and clothing, incorporates colour and textual elements to recreate sensory experiences from childhood. Riley’s artistic vision was for each piece to be a bright, fun, and tactile experience for the wearer and everyone who passes by. Colourful and captivating, Riley aims to remind the audience of the innocence and wonders of childhood.
