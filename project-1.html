Study Abroad

A visual exploration of my time abroad at the Grey School of Art located in Aberdeen Scotland.

This unfinished project yielded three repeating patterns. Each pattern attempts to capture a variety of matters and experiences related to being in a strange and wonderful new environment.

Pattern 1 - You Left Home Last Summer
Pattern 2 - Analogy
Pattern 3 - Fellow Feeling

  • About Me

    Riley Simmons is an Indigenous graphic designer working in Vancouver BC.

    Riley is a recent Capilano University graduate and is working as a graphic designer in Vancouver BC. With a diverse educational background in digital design, fashion production, and marketing from institutions like Capilano University, Robert Gordon University and Vancouver Community College, combined with impactful work experiences, Riley is passionate about connections and fostering community.
